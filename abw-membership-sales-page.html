A few things people ask before joining

Do I have to be in perimenopause to join?

Not at all. This membership is open to all women. Whether you're in your late 30s noticing early changes, navigating perimenopause or postmenopause, or simply feeling like your body needs different support than it used to, you'll find this relevant and useful.

What if I'm already on HRT? Is this still for me?

Absolutely, and this is important. HRT works best when it has a solid foundation under it. Nutrition, blood sugar stability, sleep, and stress all directly affect how well your hormones function and how effectively your body uses the support HRT provides. This membership helps you build that foundation, so everything else works the way it's supposed to.

My doctor says everything is fine. So why don't I feel fine?

This is one of the most common things I hear. Bloodwork can look normal while your body is still struggling with things like blood sugar swings, cortisol patterns, low muscle mass, and poor sleep quality. These don't always show up on standard panels, but they absolutely affect how you feel. That's exactly the gap this membership is designed to fill.

How much time does this take each week?

You can get real value from 10 to 15 minutes a day. The weekly posts are designed to be short and practical. Monthly calls are approximately 45 minutes and always available as a replay if you can't attend live.

I've tried a lot of programs. How is this different?

This isn't a program with a start and an end date. It's an ongoing coaching community. The goal is that you stop restarting and start building. Everything here is designed for the long game, not a 21-day transformation that disappears when the plan does.

Why is there a 3-month minimum?

Habits take longer than 30 days to form, and your body takes time to respond. The 3-month minimum isn't a lock-in, it's an honest reflection of how long meaningful change actually takes. After month three, you're free to stay or go.
